Hospitality Crew






The hospitality crew is a team of volunteers helping to welcome performers and
guests that visit the arts centre. The volunteers aid guests in a way that represents
Shenkman Arts Centre in Canada and the performing arts business professionally.





Want to find out more about volunteering for the Hospitality Crew?








House Crew






The House Crew helps to welcome guests by fulfilling the roles of ushers and ticket
takers. These volunteers assure the safety and security of visitors, and to represent
the theatre as ambassadors of goodwill to the general public attending performances.






Want to find out more about volunteering for the House Crew?








Stage Crew






The Stage Crew works in lighting, sound and other technical staging areas for non-profit
shows. Stage Crew volunteers don't need previous experience in theatre; volunteers
will be provided with the training needed to work efficiently, competently, and safely.
