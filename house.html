House Crew




In order to fulfill their responsabilities as ushers, ticket takers, and greeters, the members of the House Crew are provided with formal training in basic and emergency procedures.















The following are a few specific positions and responsabilities of House Crew members:



Usher / Ticket Taker


- Preparing the front of the house for patrons
- Folding and distributing programmes and advertising inserts as required
- Preventing patrons from taking beverages, cameras, and recording devices into the auditorium
- Scanning patrons' tickets
- Assisting patrons to their seats in a safe manner



Greeter


- Greeting patrons as they enter the theatre
- Assisting patrons requiring special services such as wheelchairs and elevators
- Directing patrons to the box office, coat check, rest rooms and seat locations
