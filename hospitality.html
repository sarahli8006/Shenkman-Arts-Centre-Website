Hospitality Crew







The Hospitality Crew's main responsabilities include working with staff to plan menus, shopping for ingredients, preparing meals, and serving them to visiting artists. Hospitality Crew members also assist in some marketing projects, such as customer service surveys and post show activities.



























The following are a few specific roles and responsabilities of Hospitality Crew members:
- Attending monthly crew meetings
- Planning, preparing, serving and cleaning up meals for artists and for crews throughout the course of "Shenkman Arts Centre Presents" presentations
- Promoting the theatre and its activities to the public
- Assisting at theatre events
- Assisting at Volunteer Information Sessions
