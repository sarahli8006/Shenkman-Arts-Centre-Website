Contact ontarois 2023 Showcases

Presented by Réseau Ontario

January 18 - 20, 2023

Réseau Ontario invites all to discover Francophone artists from all over
the country with these Showcases (20-minute performances followed by
an interview to learn more about the artist). Several Franco-Ontarians such
as Céleste Lévis, De Flore, Ferline Regis, JOLY, Léona, Les Bilinguish Boys,
Les Rats d'Swompe, Mclean, Mehdi Cayenne, Mitch Jean and Stéphanie
Morin-Robert will be performing.




Jersey Nights

Presented by Live Is All You Need

February 10, 2023

Jersey Nights is four superb vocalists backed with a live band to
celebrate the legendary music of Frankie Valli and the Four Seasons.
Come for the hits! Sherry, Walk Like A Man, Let’s Hang On, Working
My Way Back to You, Oh >What a Night, Big Girls Don’t Cry, Rag Doll,
(to mention just a few) … and Jersey Nights includes a mix of nostalgic
vocal hits by other artists that shaped a magical era of music.



Walter Ego

Presented by Shenkman Arts Centre and MIFO

March 15, 2023

Mr. Walter lives his life alone in his little apartment. One morning, his world is turned
upside down... Catapulted into a surrealist world, thrust into absurd twists and turns,
Walter is taken on ludicrous adventures and even runs into his Alter Ego!

Juggling literally with the world of Magritte, this clownesque and playful creation by
L’Aubergine is an invitation to enter the world of dreams where anything is possible.
Music, surprises, fantasy and jests await: happiness guaranteed!





Michelle Desrochers

Presented by MIFO

April 1, 2023

Michelle Desrochers: a highly skilled improv artist, with an extraordinary
sense of punch and a very endearing unusual face! The last few months
have been very creative and she is finally ready to break in her
one-woman-show. Dare to discover Michelle’s unusual humor in the room.
You don’t want to miss this chance.





Isn't He Lovely

Presented by Ripe Boy Productions

May 6, 2023

A celebration of the iconic Stevie Wonder, whose music has transcended
generations. Performed by Tony Nolasco and nine of Canada's finest world
class musicians and singers. An unforgettable experience!
